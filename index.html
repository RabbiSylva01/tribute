Pastor Marcello Tunasi

Biography of Pastor

Marcello Jeremie Tunasi was born on January 27, the eldest in a family of seven children, three boys and four girls, two of whom are also pastors. His light skin, as he likes to say, is the result of the mixture of his great grandparents from Germany. Originally from the village of Kabar√© in Kivu, a province of the Democratic Republic of Congo, Marcello was raised by his parents, including a father who was a former teacher and now a prefect of Kivu, where he attended elementary school before moving to Kinshasa

He was converted in 1990 following an apparition of Jesus Christ. In 1996, he received his call to the ministry. In 1998, he became the main pastor of the church La Compassion located in Kinshasa, in the Democratic Republic of Congo. In 2009, he founded the CREFM community (Center for Revival, Evangelism, Training and Mission) based on the values of Love, Holiness, Power and Balance in Jesus Christ. Passionate about Christ, he travels the world preaching his word and making disciples. Healing the sick, freeing the captives as Christ commanded in Matthew 10:8 and 28:19.

Is married to Blanche Kandolo Tunasi. They have three children. He is also the senior pastor of Compassion Church and visionary of Compassion Core International. In addition to his ministries, he is a composer of many spiritual hymns and the director of several theatrical plays. He is also a true promoter of youth: preachers, composers and Christian political leaders and a passionate supporter of student youth whom he knows to be the future of the world today.

He is also the Founder of Marcello Jeremiah Tunasi Ministries with several branches, including: The Discovery of New Ministries ( DNM): A burden that the lord had placed in the heart of his servant, whose purpose is to discover, train and mentor young preachers of the gospel in order to raise for God an army of servants with the pure and unmixed gospel. The first edition took place in September 2009, until today it goes from glory to glory. The Discovery of New Composers (DNC): whose aim is to discover new Christian composers and then promote them on the national and international scene. Nucleus Compassion International (NCI) through which actions outside national borders are carried out.
